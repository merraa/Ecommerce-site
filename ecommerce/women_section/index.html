Chiffon

Chiffon is a lightweight, sheer fabric that is often used in Ethiopian traditional dresses, especially for special occasions. In Ethiopia, traditional dresses, also known as habesha kemis (ሐበሻ ቀሚስ), are typically made from cotton, but chiffon has become a popular choice due to its elegant and flowy nature, which adds a touch of modernity to the traditional attire.
